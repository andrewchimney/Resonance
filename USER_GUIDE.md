# Resonance

Resonance is an AI-powered sound discovery platform that allows you to generate, modify, share, and explore synthesizer presets using natural language.

Live Site: https://resonance.dog/
	
No installation is required--simply visit the site in your browser.

---
## What You Can Do on Resonance
- Generate presets using text descriptions
- Refine presets through conversational feedback
- Download generated presets
- Browse and share presets with the community
- Post anonymously or with your account
- Save presets to your profile
- Customize your sound preferences

---
## Logging In
Resonance uses **Discord authentication** for secure login.

To log in:
	1. Click **"Log In"**
	2. Select **"Continue with Discord"**
	3. Authorize the application

Once logged in:
-  	Your Discord profile picture appears in the navigation bar
- Clicking it opens your **Account Panel**
- From there, you can:
-- View your profile
-- Sign out.

If you are not logged in, some features (such as preset generation) will be restricted.

---
## AI Preset Generation
Navigate to the **Generation** Page.

If logged out:
- The interface appears blurred
- You will be prompted to log in

If logged in:
- The chat input becomes active
- You can begin describing the sound you want  

---
## Generate a Preset
1. Type a description 
	(example: *"Give me a fat bass"*)
2. Click **"Send"**
3. Resonance generates five preset suggestions

Each preset includes:
- Title
- Match percentage	
- Audio preview
- Download option

---
## Refining a Preset
After selecting a preset, you can continue modifying it.

Examples:
- "*Make it darker.*"
- "*Increase brightness slightly.*"
- "*Decrease the agressiveness*"

Resonance will:
- Update supported parameters
- Explain what was changed
- Allow continued playback
- Keep the download option available

You may redownload the preset after refining it.

---
## Browse & Posting
The **Browse** page displays presets shared by the community.
- Posts are ordered from most recent to oldest.
- You can explore what others have created.

## Creating a Post
Click the **"+" button** in the top-right corner.

You can choose to:
#### 1. Post anonymously
- Requires a title
- Optional description
- Optional preset upload
- Not linked to a profile

#### 2. Post While Logged In
- Linked to your profile
- Display your username
- Appears in your "Your Posts" section of the **Profile** page

---
## Community Interaction
Each post allows users to:
- Upvote
- Downvote
- Open the Comment Panel

When the comment button is clicked:
- A section titled **"Comments from the Community"** appears
- Comments can be sorted by:
-- Most Recent
-- Most Relevant
-- Most Interacted

(Note: Comment submission functionality is currently limited.)

---
## Profile Page
Accessed your profile by clicking your profile picture and selecting **"View Profile"**.

The profile page includes:

### I. User Search
- Search for other users
- View their join date
- View posts they have created

### II. Saved presets
- View presets you have saved
- If none exist, you will see:
	"*No presets found. You haven't saved any presets yet.*"

### III. Account Information
- View presets you have saved
- If none exist, you will see:
- See your "Member Since" date

### IV. Preferences
- Describe your preferred sound styles
- Pre-filled guidance text helps you write useful preferences

### V. Your Posts
- View posts created by your account
- If none exist, you will see:
	*"No posts found. No posts are associated with this accout yet."*

---
## Current Limitations
- Comment submission is not fully operational.
- Advanced filtering and recommendation systems are still in development.
- Preset generation may be limited dependening on system usage.

---
## Summary
Resonance combines AI-assisted sound generation with a social discovery platform. 

You can:
- Generate sounds from text
- Refine them conversationally
- Download and save presets
- Share sound creation with the community
- Explore what others have built

Resonance is designed to make sound design more accessible, interactive, and collaborative.
